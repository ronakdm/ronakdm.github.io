About

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Statistics at the University of Washington. Previously, I was an undergraduate and Master's student in the Department of Applied Mathemathics and Statistics at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Joshua T. Vogelstein and Carey Priebe.

My past research projects spanned nonparametric estimation and hypothesis testing, continual/lifelong machine learning, and functional connectomics applications.

I am also passionate about modern statistics and data science education, and am committed to maintaining a safe and inclusive environment for my students and colleagues.
